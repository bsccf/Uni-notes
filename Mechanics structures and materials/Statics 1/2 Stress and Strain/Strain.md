#### What's
## Strain
Note that for non uniform strains [[local strain]] should be used.

This is the ratio of extention compared to origional length.

> $$ \epsilon =  \frac{\Delta l}{l} $$ 
>> where:
>> $\epsilon=$ strain 
>> $\Delta l=$ extention
>> $l=$ origional length

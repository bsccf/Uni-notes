#### What is
## First form of the [[second law of thermodynamics]]

> ### $$ Q_H = Q_C + W $$ 
>> where:
>> $Q_H=$ heat from hot reservoir
>> $Q_C=$ heat lost to cold reservoir
>> $W=$ useful work out
>> $Q_C > 0$ (from the [[second law of thermodynamics]])

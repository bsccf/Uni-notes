#### What is a
## Square matrix
This is just a [[matrices (maths)|matrix]] that has an equal number of rows to columns.

### Examples
$$ \begin{pmatrix} 
a
\end{pmatrix} $$

$$ \begin{pmatrix} 
a & b \\ c & d
 \end{pmatrix} $$
 
 $$ \begin{pmatrix} 
a & b & c \\ d & e & f  \\ g & h & i
\end{pmatrix} $$

$$ \begin{pmatrix} 
a & b & c & d \\ e &  f & g & h \\ i & j & j & k \\ m & n & o & p
\end{pmatrix} $$

(I think you get the idea)
